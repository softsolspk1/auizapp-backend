# Hilton Quiz App - MVP Client Summary

## 🎯 **Project Overview**
The Hilton Quiz App is a comprehensive medical knowledge competition platform designed specifically for healthcare professionals. This MVP demonstrates both the **Web Admin Panel** and **Mobile App** with complete functionality and professional branding.

## 🎨 **Design & Branding**
- **Logo Integration**: Hilton Quiz logo prominently displayed on all screens
- **Color Scheme**: Professional medical color palette matching logo colors:
  - Primary Blue: #1e40af
  - Medical Green: #059669  
  - Professional Cyan: #06b6d4
- **Zeegap Branding**: Integrated in footer of key screens and admin sidebar
- **Modern UI**: Clean, professional interface suitable for medical professionals

## 📱 **Mobile App Features**
### **Authentication & Registration**
- ✅ Splash screen with auto-navigation
- ✅ Login with email/password
- ✅ Complete doctor registration with PMDC verification
- ✅ Forgot password functionality
- ✅ All screens feature Hilton Quiz logo

### **Core Functionality**
- ✅ Home dashboard with user statistics
- ✅ Category-based quiz selection
- ✅ Single player mode (+10 points correct, -5 wrong)
- ✅ Multiplayer mode (+20 points correct, -5 wrong)
- ✅ Interactive quiz interface with timer
- ✅ Results screen with detailed analytics
- ✅ Leaderboard with top performers
- ✅ Profile management with statistics

### **User Experience**
- ✅ Intuitive navigation with bottom menu
- ✅ Smooth animations and transitions
- ✅ Professional medical color scheme
- ✅ Responsive design for all devices
