# Hilton Quiz App - Complete MVP Summary

## 🎯 **Project Overview**
The Hilton Quiz App MVP is now a comprehensive, fully-featured demonstration of a medical knowledge competition platform. This MVP showcases **100% of the app features** with complete logo integration and professional branding.

- ✅ **Full Logo Display**: Complete Hilton Quiz logo shown properly (not cropped) on all screens
- ✅ **Logo Colors**: Professional medical color scheme matching logo colors
- ✅ **Zeegap Branding**: Integrated in footer of splash, login, signup, forgot password screens, and admin panel sidebar
- ✅ **Consistent Branding**: Logo and branding elements throughout the entire application

## 📱 **Mobile App - 100% Features**

### **Authentication & Onboarding**
- ✅ **Splash Screen**: Auto-navigation with full logo and Zeegap footer
- ✅ **Login Screen**: Email/password with logo and Zeegap footer
- ✅ **Registration Screen**: Complete doctor registration with PMDC verification, logo and Zeegap footer
- ✅ **Forgot Password**: Password reset with logo and Zeegap footer

### **Core App Features**
- ✅ **Home Dashboard**: User stats, recent activity, quick actions, category grid
- ✅ **Quiz Categories**: 6 medical specialties (Neuro Surgery, Neurology, Peads Neurology, Surgery, Emergency, Psychiatry)
- ✅ **Single Player Mode**: Practice quizzes with standard scoring (+10/-5 points)
- ✅ **Multiplayer Mode**: Real-time competition with double scoring (+20/-5 points)
- ✅ **Waiting Room**: Player management, chat system, room settings
- ✅ **Quiz Interface**: Interactive questions with timer and scoring
- ✅ **Results Screen**: Detailed performance analysis with points earned
- ✅ **Leaderboard**: Top performers ranking system
- ✅ **Profile Management**: Complete user information, statistics, achievements
- ✅ **Settings Screen**: Notifications, game settings, privacy, account management
- ✅ **Achievement System**: Badges and milestones tracking
- ✅ **Category Performance**: Individual specialty statistics

### **Advanced Features**
- ✅ **Recent Activity**: User activity feed with achievements
- ✅ **Room Creation**: Custom multiplayer rooms with settings
- ✅ **Available Rooms**: Browse and join existing multiplayer games
- ✅ **Chat System**: Real-time messaging in waiting rooms
