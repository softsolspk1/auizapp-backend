🏥 Specialty Quiz Features

Enhanced Quiz Experience with 30-Second Timer & Detailed Results

⏱️ 30-Second Timer with Visual Feedback

Each question has a 30-second countdown timer with color-coded visual feedback:

25s
Green: 20+ seconds (plenty of time)
15s
Yellow: 10-20 seconds (moderate urgency)
5s
Red: <10 seconds (urgent - pulsing animation)

📊 Detailed Results with Wrong Answer Review

After completing the quiz, users can review their wrong answers with detailed explanations:

Question 3: Which specialist would you consult for a skin rash?

Your Answer:
Cardiologist
Correct Answer:
Dermatologist
Explanation:
Dermatologists specialize in conditions affecting the skin, hair, and nails. They are the medical specialists you should consult for skin rashes, moles, and other dermatological conditions.
